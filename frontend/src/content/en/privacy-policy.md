# Privacy Policy

Updated December 28, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **ft_transcendence**, operated by **The Transcenders** as part of the 42 School curriculum.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Pong tournament platform.

By using ft_transcendence, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Account information

When you create an account, we collect the following data:

- Email address: for account identification and authentication
- Username: displayed to other users
- Password: stored as an encrypted hash, we never see your actual password
- Profile avatar/picture, if you choose to upload one.

### 2.2 Authentication data

We support multiple authentication methods:

- Email/Password authentication with securely stored credentials
- Google OAuth, by receiving limited profile information from Google
- Two-Factor authentication codes through manually enabling two-factor authentication from your account.

### 2.3 Game and platform data

We automatically collect and store:

- Game statistics: wins, losses, and match results
- Tournament participation and results
- Match history: scores, games played, and opponents
- Friends list: connections to other users
- Online status: when you're actively using the platform.

### 2.4 Technical data

We collect minimal technical information:

- Store data in local storage, essential for keeping users logged in
- Browser type and version for compatibility
- Connection timestamps for security purposes.

We do not collect:

- Detailed browsing behavior or analytics
- Marketing or advertising data
- Payment information, as this is a free platform.

## 3. How we use your information

We use your personal information for:

### 3.1 Platform operation

- Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
- Enable matchmaking between players
- Organize and manage tournaments
- Display game statistics
- Facilitate friend connections between users.

### 3.2 Platform improvement

- Monitor platform performance and stability
- Debug technical issues
- Improve game mechanics and user experience.

### 3.3 Security

- Prevent cheating and exploitation
- Detect and prevent unauthorized access
- Protect against abuse and violations of our Terms of Service.

## 4. How we store and protect your information

### 4.1 Data storage

- All data is stored locally on 42 School infrastructure
- Data is not transferred to third-party servers (except for Google OAuth authentication)
- Database access is restricted to The Transcenders development team.

### 4.2 Security measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

- Encrypted passwords: all passwords are hashed using secure algorithms
- HTTPS encryption: all data transmission is encrypted
- Two-factor authentication: available to optionally enable for enhanced security

However, as this is an educational project, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a unique password and enable 2FA for additional protection.

## 5. Third-party services

### 5.1 Google OAuth

If you choose to authenticate via Google OAuth:

- We receive basic profile information (email, name) from Google
- Your Google password is never shared with us
- Google's data handling is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- You can revoke ft_transcendence's access via your Google Account settings.

### 5.2 42 School

As this is a school project:

- 42 School staff may access platform data for evaluation purposes
- Evaluators may review user accounts, game functionality, and stored data
- This access is limited to educational assessment.

## 6. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

- With your consent, if applicable
- To 42 School evaluators for project assessment
- When required and obligated by law.

Your game statistics and match history may be visible to other users on leaderboards and in match histories.

## 7. Your privacy rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 7.1 Access

You can view your profile information, game statistics, and match history at any time through your account.

### 7.2 Correction

You can update your username, email, and avatar through account settings.

## 8. Data retention

We retain your data until the project has been completed.

### 9 Project lifespan

ft_transcendence is a temporary school project. All user accounts and data will be permanently deleted at the end of the third evaluation period.

## 10. Children's privacy

ft_transcendence is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

## 11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of ft_transcendence after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 12. Contact us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at hello@transcendence.fi.
